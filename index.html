About Us



We are The House of Creative Indians.
Now answering the question in your mind,"How are WE any more creative than YOU are?" Trust me, we are not.

We are artists in our hearts and what feeds our soul is the raw passion to simply reach out and create an aggregation of people like us. We want to deliver the best in the particular profession to cater for your development.We WANT you to ace at whatever you think you are good at. We always have had an itch to meet people like ourselves; to sit down for a cup of coffee and discuss our passions.Wouldn't it be incredible? A house where creativity is all we yearn for.

House of Creative Indians is a setup for building a unified creative base in India and for Indians outside India.The foundation of the House was laid by a group of youngsters who have mended their ways to become professionally eligible for top notch IT/Technical jobs but still believe in their passions of becoming culturally rich and thereby monetizing and letting every associate earn royalty for their respective shared art.

The division of the House is in the form of Creative Executives, Creative Associates/Members and Mentors.

Membership



Learning any form of art is not limited to books.
It is an extensive process encompassing series of lectures, seminars, workshops, field visits and practical learning.

But, HCI believes that learning Liberal Arts is not a one-life-process.
So we see to it that dreams take shape with every individual learning the skills at a youthful age.

The three levels which let the members grow along with the House of Creative Indians are:

1. Junior Creative Associate
2. Senior creative Associate
3. Artiste

Membership Advantages:


Get trained and guided by industry professionals.
Become a part of all the workshops conducted by HCI.
Actively coordinate all the workshops for HCI.
Keep yourself updated about the regional, national, international activities/competitions.

Become eligible for participation in stage shows or events coordinated by HCI.

Earn and become qualified trainers.
Engage actively in imparting Liberal Arts training in and outside the country.

Be a part of the Client Service Team as Consulting Professionals from HCI.

Be a part of the production unit of the films produced under the aegis of HCI.

Independent Research funding from HCI.

The Artiste's fort

It takes more than one life to master any form of art and Ulysses taught us the same when referring to knowledge.

"For it is not how you learn the art, but from whom you learn it. Knowledge is a deadly weapon, if only taught by the right master."

The artiste's of the House of Creative Indians understand the integrity of teaching the young ones and are inculcating their experiences in the form of Once-In-A-Lifetime Workshops. The workshops are designed to cater to all forms of creativity. The diverse forms of learning art are practiced using state-of-the-art learning gears.The trained industry professionals gather at one place and share the essentials of different forms of art.

Is it theatre? Is it Fine Arts? Is it Music? Is it Sand Art? Is it the codes for Angry Birds? House of Creative Indians has got every form of artiste's for every willing learner. We let the inhabitants ignite the fire of desire to learn, implement and achieve for a glorious tomorrow.

The Film-makers Room

At the end of the day we are all but stories. Some unique, some common but every one is a character and every character is a part of this small story called Life. Films have given us a way to look back to those stories. We believe motion picture has got the maximum power to shape the way a society thinks. To cultivate this thought we have given preference to the dwellers of this room of the house. To cater to the most versatile form of art i.e. Film-making, we bring to you the exceptionally talented film -makers.

From the very ripe English Tales to the real life Indian Myths, the lights shine on those stories which are still unheard and unseen.
From the edging French Narrative to the Musical Indian dholaks, the camera revolves on those scripts which have the power to move the masses.
From the war-fevers of Roman battlefields to the collieries of India's east, action is yelled on plots which have the feel of the homeland.
